Planets

Planets are celestial bodies that orbit a star, such as our Sun. They come in a variety of sizes, compositions, and distances from their host stars. Some are rocky, like Earth and Mars, while others are gas giants, like Jupiter and Saturn. Their diverse atmospheres, climates, and physical characteristics make them fascinating objects of study in astronomy and science exploration.
